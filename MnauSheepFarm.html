Our Specialty

Breeds We Raise

We specialise in breeds suited for wool, meat, dairy and dual-purpose farming across Karnataka.

🐑

Merino

World's finest wool. Soft dense fleece ideal for textile markets.

🐏

Dorper

Fast-growing meat breed. Hardy and adaptable to Karnataka's climate.

🐑

Deccani

Native Karnataka breed. Tough, well-adapted to local conditions.

🐏

Bannur

Famous Karnataka meat breed, popular for its tender flavour.

🐑

Suffolk

Popular meat breed with excellent growth rates and good muscling.

🐏

Corriedale

Dual-purpose breed with medium-fine wool and solid meat yields.
